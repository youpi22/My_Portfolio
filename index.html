My Project

01.

Pamana
Experience Philippine culture in a fun and interactive way with PAMANA!
PAMANA takes players on a journey across different levels inspired by Philippine traditions, history, and folklore. Each stage combines engaging cutscenes with fun mini-games—such as puzzles, memory pairs, quizzes, and word searches—that challenge players while teaching cultural knowledge. With its blend of learning and entertainment, PAMANA aims to preserve and promote Filipino heritage in a modern, interactive way.
Role: Project Lead and Game Developer.

02.

Service Record
“Efficient system for accurate and seamless employee records.”
The Service Record System is built to streamline the way organizations manage employee records by providing a secure, accurate, and user-friendly platform. It eliminates the hassle of manual paperwork, reduces errors, and ensures that all service history is easily accessible when needed. With its automated features and organized structure, the system boosts productivity, saves time, and enhances transparency—making record management more convenient and dependable for both employees and administrators.
Role: Intern, System Developer.

03.

GO-LAY
Go-Lay Fresh vegetables delivered fast and hassle-free!
Go-Lay is an e-commerce app designed to bring farm-fresh vegetables straight to your doorstep. With just a few taps, customers can enjoy healthy, high-quality produce without the stress of going to the market. Convenient, reliable, and affordable—Go-Lay makes eating fresh easier than ever.
Role: Development, Testing, Project management, Training.
